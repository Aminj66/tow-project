About Us

The inspiration for the Towing and Recovery System came from my personal experience of facing delays and inefficiencies when my car broke down on a remote highway. I realized the need for a better way to manage towing and recovery services, ensuring timely assistance and better communication between service providers and customers.

This project is a part of my Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I am learning full-stack web development. Through this project, I aim to showcase my skills and provide a practical solution to a common problem. You can find more details and follow the development process on my GitHub repository.
